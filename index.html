DISCOVER THE BIG FIVE

ELEPHANT

Elephants, the largest land mammals, possess massive bodies, large ears, long trunks—essential for tasks like picking objects, trumpeting, and drinking.

LION

Lions, the kings of the jungle, possess powerful bodies, sharp teeth, and claws for hunting and dominating their prey.

RHINO

Rhinoceroses, with thick skin, short legs, and one or two horns, are large herbivores that solely consume plants.

LEOPARD

Leopards, robust and agile, have long bodies, short legs, and a broad head. They climb trees and store prey in branches.

BUFFALO

Savanna buffaloes, cow-like and hefty, exhibit diverse sizes, horn shapes, and coloring. The African buffalo is renowned as a perilous animal to hunt by foot
